There are over 5 trillion pieces of plastic in our oceans.

That's a lot of plastic.

We’re on a mission to help our oceans by discussing the effects of our plastic waste. We believe that being aware of what is happening is the first step to helping out. That’s why we’ve created this website with facts, links to other sources of information, an interactive quiz and the chance to sign up to our free monthly newsletter.

Our Oceans, Our Plastic is a non-profit organisation that aims to educate, inspire and empower each of us to know how we can make a real difference to the health and future of our oceans.
